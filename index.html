Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

At Lovely Words, your privacy is a core value. We believe that an app dedicated to love and vocabulary should be a safe space. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

Lovely Words is designed to function with minimal data collection. We do not require you to create an account to use the basic features of the app.

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous analytics (e.g., which features are used most) to improve the app experience.
  • Personal Input: Any words or favorites you save stay locally on your device unless synced via your private iCloud account.

2. Data Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your iOS device. If you use Apple's iCloud sync features, your data is encrypted and stored in your personal Apple account. Patara Labs does not have access to your private vocabulary lists or notes.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use Apple's standard developer tools (like TestFlight or App Store Connect analytics) which help us understand app performance and crashes.

4. Your Rights

Since we do not store your personal data on our own servers, you have full control over your data through your device settings and your Apple ID account.
