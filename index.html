Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Love Words does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.

1. Data Collection

Love Words does not collect any data. The app is designed to work entirely offline without requiring any personal information from you.

  • No analytics: We do not use any analytics or tracking services.
  • No accounts: No registration or sign-in is required.
  • No network requests: The app does not connect to the internet.

2. Local Storage

Love Words stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Bookmarked words: Your saved favorite words are stored on your device.
  • App preferences: Settings like whether you've completed the walkthrough.

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to Patara Labs or any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

Love Words does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or advertising networks. The only external component is the Lottie animation library, which operates entirely offline.

4. Your Data, Your Control

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Deleting the app removes all associated data.
  • You have complete control over your information.
  • No data recovery is possible by Patara Labs after deletion.

5. Children's Privacy

Love Words is safe for users of all ages. We do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new version on this page with an updated revision date.
